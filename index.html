TEN HUNDRED

Artist, Designer,Creator of Colorful New Worlds

Ten Hundred (Peter Robinson) is an artist from Seattle, WA. He was born and raised in Michigan.
He specializes in bright, colorful,imaginative character work. He has shown work in Seattle, Portland, Denver and Los Angeles. He has murals in Seattle, New York City, Los Angeles, Kansas City, Tokyo Japan and Buzios Brazil.
He is inspired by cartoons and anime, street art and graffiti, childlike imagination, comics, world cultures, religions, and folklore. He spends his time creating fine art, YouTube videos, clothing, toys and figurines, designing art for businesses, and creating large murals all around the world.
